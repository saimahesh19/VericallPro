Understanding Scams & FedEx Scam Detection

A scam is a deceptive scheme designed to trick individuals into providing sensitive information or money. Scammers employ various techniques, including impersonation, social engineering, and AI-generated fraud, to exploit victims.

How and Why Scams Are Created?

Scammers operate to gain financial benefits, steal identities, or extract confidential data. They manipulate victims through urgency, fear tactics, and trust exploitation.

How Phone Call Scams Work?

  • Impersonation: Scammers pose as trusted companies (banks, FedEx, government agencies).
  • Urgency & Threats: False claims about account security issues, package delays, or legal notices.
  • Phishing for Data: Requests for OTPs, banking details, or login credentials.
  • Voice Phishing (Vishing): AI-generated voices convincingly mimic real representatives.

What is FedEx?

FedEx is a global courier service handling millions of packages daily. Due to its trusted reputation, scammers often exploit it to conduct fraud through fake delivery notices and payment requests.

Why Develop an Application for FedEx Scam Detection?

FedEx-related scams are on the rise, tricking people into revealing personal data. Our application helps detect and prevent fraud by analyzing call audio, identifying scam keywords, and flagging suspicious activity.

How Our FedEx Scam Detection Works?

  • Speech Analysis: Uses NLP to detect fraudulent call content.
  • Voice Fingerprinting: Identifies deepfake or AI-generated voices.
  • Spectrogram Processing: Analyzes voice spectrograms for anomalies.
  • Real-time Alerts: Notifies users of potential scams.

Our AI-driven detection system helps users stay ahead of evolving scam tactics.
